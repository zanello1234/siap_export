üìù How It Works

Step 1: Navigate to the SIAP Export module in your Odoo instance
Step 2: Launch the export wizard from the main menu
Step 3: Select export type (Accounts Receivable or Payable)
Step 4: Choose your date range for the report
Step 5: Click "Export" to generate your SIAP-compliant TXT file
Step 6: Download and submit to government authorities
